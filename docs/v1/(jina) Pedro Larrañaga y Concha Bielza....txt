Pedro Larrañaga y Concha Bielza, premiados por la Amity University (India)
==========================================================================

![Image 7: India3](https://muia.dia.fi.upm.es/wp-content/uploads/sites/6/2020/01/India3.jpg)

Los profesores del MUIA Pedro Larrañaga y Concha Bielza, reciben el Premio otorgado por Amity University, en Noida (India) por su relevante contribución investigadora en Machine Learning.

Los catedráticos e investigadores de la ETSIINF, Pedro Larrañaga y Concha Bielza, han recibido  el Premio «Amity Researcher Award for Significant Contribution in the Field of Machine Learning» en Confluence-2020: 10th International Conference on Cloud Computing, Data Science & Engineering ([https://www.amity.edu/aset/confluence2020/](https://www.amity.edu/aset/confluence2020/)). Amity University en Noida (India) les ha hecho entrega de este Premio.

### International Campus of Excellence in Innovation

![Image 12: Campus Montegancedo](https://www.fi.upm.es/web/futuroalumno/wp-content/uploads/sites/15/2015/11/logo_i2tech.png)

The School is located on the Montegancedo Campus,  
which has been **designated by the Ministry of Education**  
**as an International Campus of Excellence in Innovation**,  
the only one in Spain with this recognition.

![Image 13](https://www.fi.upm.es/web/futuroalumno/wp-content/uploads/sites/15/2015/11/logo_academic-ranking.jpg)

Science (out of over  
15,000 universities).
